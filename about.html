Our Story

Konnichiwa! We're a husband-and-wife team living in the vibrant city of Osaka, Japan.

ShogunShip isn't a big corporation or a traditional store. We're just a small, passionate pop-up shop dedicated to sharing the incredible finds and unique culture of Japan with the world.

Think of us as your personal treasure hunters! Whether it's a rare collectible, a trending fashion item, or something uniquely Japanese you can't find anywhere else, we're here to help you get it.

This little venture helps us pay the bills while doing something we truly love: connecting people worldwide with Japanese treasures. We're always excited to hear what specific items you're looking for.

Feel free to reach out anytime; we're always happy to help bridge the distance and bring a piece of Japan to your doorstep.
